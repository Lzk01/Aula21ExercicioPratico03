About

Endangered animals are those threatened with disappearing from Earth. Deforestation, climate change, illegal
hunting and animal trafficking are some of the reasons that have put many animals on the verge of extinction.
Despite being relatively common in nature, the extinction process is being intensified by human action.

A report developed by the United Nations (UN) concluded that around one million species are at risk of extinction,
which reinforces the need to create more conservation programs.
